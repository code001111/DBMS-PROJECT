## ğŸ“ How to Use

### Step 1: Browse Products
1. Click "Shop" in navigation
2. View all products with prices and stock

### Step 2: Add to Cart
1. Select quantity for desired product
2. Click "Add to Cart"
3. Cart count updates in navbar

### Step 3: Review Cart
1. Go to "Cart" page
2. See all items with prices
3. Update quantities or remove items
4. View total with shipping cost

### Step 4: Checkout & Place Order
1. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
2. Fill customer details:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Address
   - City
3. Review order summary
4. Click "Place Order"

### Step 5: Get Bill
1. After order placed, auto-redirected to bill page
2. See complete invoice with:
   - Order ID
   - Customer details
   - Itemized list
   - Total amount
3. Click "Print Bill" to generate PDF

### Step 6: View Your Orders
1. Go to "Orders" page
2. Enter your Customer ID (shown on bill)
3. See all your orders
4. Click "View Bill" to see any invoice
